# Retention Insights

## Executive Summary

The analysis covers 2,500 users and shows a latest DAU of 61, latest MAU of 369, and DAU/MAU stickiness of 16.5%.

Average Month 1 retention is 68.4%; average Month 3 retention is 44.5%. The latest observed monthly churn rate is 18.5%.

## Cohort Retention

Signup cohorts retain best in the first month, then decline steadily through Month 3 and Month 6. The most recent six cohorts have average Month 1 retention of 70.2%, which should be monitored as the leading indicator for long-term retention.

## Engagement Drop-Off

The largest early lifecycle risk is before the first key action:

- Onboarding completion rate: 74.8%
- First key action conversion rate: 50.6%
- Drop-off before first key action: 49.4%

By channel, `Social` has the weakest first-key-action conversion at 43.5%, while `Referral` performs best at 65.6%.

## Recommendations

1. Improve the first-session activation path with a shorter onboarding flow and clearer next best action.
2. Trigger lifecycle emails or in-app nudges for users who sign up but do not complete onboarding within 24 hours.
3. Create channel-specific onboarding for lower-converting acquisition sources, especially `Social`.
4. Study successful behaviors from `Referral` users and reuse those prompts in weaker channels.
5. Track Month 1 retention as the north-star early retention metric, with supporting KPIs for onboarding completion and first key action conversion.
